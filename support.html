How do I cancel my subscription?

All subscriptions are managed through Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Cloudlist → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

In Cloudlist, open Settings → Delete Account. This permanently erases all your tasks, streaks, history, and account data from our servers. This action can’t be undone.

How do I restore a purchase?

If you’ve subscribed before and your subscription isn’t showing, open Cloudlist → Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases.

How do I export my data?

Email cloudlistsupport@gmail.com and we’ll help you export it.
